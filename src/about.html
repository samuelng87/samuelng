About Me

Melbourne

I have completed my Bachelor of Information Technology at KENT Institute Australia in March 2023, equipping me with the skills to develop web information systems using various technologies.

I have also completed my ACS Professional Year at Monash Melbourne and remain committed to staying at the forefront of emerging web technologies through continuous self-learning via platforms like Udemy and YouTube.

I gained three months of internship experience as a Software Engineer at Beyond Zero Technologies, an Australian company specializing in building technology solutions for sustainability and efficiency. This experience not only strengthened my technical skills but also expanded my interest in Business Analysis and Project Management by exposing me to project workflows, stakeholder collaboration, and business process improvements. Additionally, I volunteered in an agile environment at Treiner, demonstrating adaptability and teamwork.

I am now actively seeking entry-level roles and internships in Web Development, ICT Business Analysis, and ICT Project Management to apply my skills and contribute to a dynamic team. Feel free to reach out for any inquiries or collaboration opportunities.
