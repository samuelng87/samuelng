My Reflective Journal

In 2017, my late adolescence marked one of the lowest points in my life, where I faced significant stress in various areas such as career, relationships, and personal failures.

After recognizing the importance of admitting ignorance, "Kaizen" Continuous Improvement played a crucial role in achieving my milestones. Below is a fraction my of life experiences acquired during career, relationships, and other aspects of my life.

Perhaps expressing everything in words could serve as the key vehicle for strengthening my cognitive connection, logical thinking about how we feel and act. This way, learning becomes about the entirety of who we are, not just a fragmented part.
